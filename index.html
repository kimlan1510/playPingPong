Lets Play Ping Pong

Decimal Ping Pong

Ping pong will count up to your number from with the following exceptions:

  1. Numbers divisible by 3 become "ping"
  2. Numbers divisibe by 5 become "pong"
  3. numbers divisible by 3 and 5 become "ping-pong"

    Binary Ping Pong

    Ping pong will convert your number to a correspondent binary number:

    1. Number 1 becomes "ping"
    2. Number 0 becomes "pong"
